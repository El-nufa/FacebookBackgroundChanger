Facebook Page Opacity:

The lower the number, the more you can see through Facebook pages.

Background Sharing


In public background mode, anyone else with this extension installed will see your background when visiting your profile and vice versa. You will not be able to see other peoples backgrounds if you are in private mode. In public mode, you're background will be uploaded to our servers a maximum of once every 15 minutes.

Background Width


Static Mode - Backgrounds will not change size and they will repeat horizontally and vertically.
Automatic Mode - Backgrounds will be the same width as your screen, and adjust as you resize the window.
